About Alpacas Production

Alpacas Production is the next-generation agency redefining the electronic music industry. We are not just a booking agency — we are a global platform that empowers DJs and producers to reach their full potential. Through cutting-edge technology, strategic marketing, and deep industry connections, we create powerful career trajectories for artists.

Our mission is to connect emerging talent with the world's biggest clubs, festivals, and brands, elevating them to the global stage.

Our Core Values:

  • Artist-Centric Approach — Every career is unique, and we craft personalized growth strategies.
  • Cutting-Edge Technology — AI-driven analytics, automated booking, Web3 solutions, and NFT monetization.
  • Global Reach & Vision — We operate beyond local markets, focusing on worldwide success.
  • Creative & Strategic Branding — Helping artists build their brand, content, and loyal fanbase.
  • Community & Collaboration — Alpacas Production is more than an agency; it’s a movement of talent, promoters, and brands shaping the future of electronic music.
  • One Family, One Vision — At Alpacas Production, every DJ is part of a family. We believe in supporting, uplifting, and growing together.

Why Alpacas Production?

  • Exclusive industry connections with top festivals and clubs worldwide.
  • Expert artist development — marketing, PR, social media, and brand partnerships.
  • Advanced booking & management — negotiating the best deals and crafting sustainable careers.
  • Education & mentorship — guiding DJs not just to perform but to evolve as artists and global brands.

Alpacas Production — Where the Future of Electronic Music Begins!
